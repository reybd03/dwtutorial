Sites

Among the various features that Dreamweaver provides to the designer, creating and managing sites is essential when wanting to group media, files and other necessities belonging to your page/Web site.

So how do I Build a Site?

To create a site, first create a new folder on your hard drive, and insert any media or files that will be a part of your web site. After that, go to Site | Manage Sites | New, which will bring you to a window where you can enter the details for your site

From the window enter the new site name, as well as the location of the folder that you just created

Once that is done, Dreamweaver will display the folder contents via the Files panel, in which linking and attaching files and media can be done with ease.
